Chapter 1

The Emperor sat above the silent court and asked why the northern garrisons had requested more grain and silver.

Director Chen answered that the spy bureau had found mismatched reports from the Ministry of War. He did not accuse anyone directly. Instead, he asked whether the ministry had counted the cost of winter patrols.

Young Lord Fan noticed that the Emperor was watching him rather than Director Chen. He joked that an empty granary could not be filled with loyal speeches, but he did not claim to know the Emperor's private plan.

The Crown Prince supported immediate funding. The Second Prince proposed another inspection, turning the military question into a struggle over who controlled the investigation.
